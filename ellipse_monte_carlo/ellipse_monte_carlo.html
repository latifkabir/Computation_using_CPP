ELLIPSE_MONTE_CARLO
Monte Carlo Estimate of Integrals in an Ellipse

ELLIPSE_MONTE_CARLO is a C++ library which uses the Monte Carlo method to estimate the value of integrals over the interior of an ellipse in 2D.
